# Product Context: PharmaNet MyShop

## Purpose
Provide a localized e-commerce solution for pharmaceutical products in Malaysia, addressing:
- Regulatory compliance for pharmaceutical sales
- Malaysian-specific payment and delivery needs
- Multi-language support (English/Malay)
- Role-based access for different stakeholders

## User Experience Goals
### Consumers
- Easy browsing and purchasing of pharmaceutical products
- Malaysian-localized experience (currency, addresses, etc.)
- Secure checkout and order tracking
- Prescription upload capability (future)

### Vendors
- Product management dashboard
- Order fulfillment tools
- Sales analytics
- Inventory management

### Admins
- User management
- Content moderation
- System configuration
- Reporting

## Key Differentiators
- Built specifically for Malaysian pharmaceutical market
- Integrated with Malaysian payment gateways
- Compliant with local pharmaceutical regulations
- Support for nationwide delivery tracking
